# Quick Task: Early Bird Pricing

**Task:** Implementasi pricing tier "100 pembeli pertama" dengan harga spesial Rp 4.900, otomatis naik ke Rp 49.000 setelah kuota habis.

**Changes:**
1. **config/products.php** — Tambah `early_bird_price` (4900) dan `early_bird_limit` (100), serta `price_normal` (490000), `price_discount` (49000), `price_special` (4900)
2. **OrderController** — Logic pricing server-side: hitung `Order::count()`, jika < limit → early bird price, else → regular price. Prevents price tampering.
3. **LandingPageController** — Pass `$isEarlyBird`, `$remainingSlots`, `$earlyBirdLimit`, `$price`, `$priceNormal`, `$priceDiscount`, `$priceSpecial` ke view
4. **landing.blade.php** — Badge "PROMO 100 PEMBELI PERTAMA — Sisa X slot" di hero dan pricing section. 3-tier price comparison UI.
5. **LeadMagnetController** — Pass `$currentPrice` ke lead magnet view (dynamic pricing)
6. **lead-magnet/show.blade.php** — Upsell price dinamis (bukan hardcoded Rp 49.000)

**Config Values:**
- `EARLY_BIRD_PRICE=4900`
- `EARLY_BIRD_LIMIT=100`
- `PRODUCT_PRICE=49000` (regular)
- `price_normal=490000`
- `price_discount=49000`
- `price_special=4900`
